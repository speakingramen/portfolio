An adventurer in design.

I am a freelance illustrator and UX designer while being an art teacher to students of all ages at Cornerstone Art and Music. I am always curious on how design moves people's emotions and thoughts and how it can be interpreted differently from person to person.

Teaching art and interacting with people from different vast range of ages and background has opened my eyes into accessibility in UX design. My illustrations' main focus is driving a story or a vision to my clients and provide the upmost care to my craftsmanship.

I studied illustration at SCAD for 3 years and while freelancing and teaching; joined into the George Washington University UX/UI Bootcamp course.

I am currently exploring on Axure and 3D programs such as Blender, Zbrush and Sketchup. As well as working on frontend development in web design.
